What is a VPN ?

A VPN or virtual private network,is a secure tunnel b/w your device and internet.
VPNs protect you from snooping, and cencorship. !!


A VPN (virtual private network) is the easiest and most effective way for
people to protect their internet traffic and keep their identities private online.
As you connect to a secure VPN server VPN server,your internet traffic goes
through an encrypted tunnel that nobody can see into,including hackers,
governments,and your internet service provider.
